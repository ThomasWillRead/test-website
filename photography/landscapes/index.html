The Lake District

The Lake District is a mountainous region in northwest England, located in the county of Cumbria

It is England’s largest national park and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its dramatic landscapes of glacial lakes, rugged fells, and deep valleys

These pictures are from my Gold DofE expedition of the picturesque landscapes that make for an incredible and beautiful challenge

Dartmoor

Dartmoor is a national park located in Devon, southwest England, covering approximately 954 square kilometers (368 square miles)

It is known for its rugged terrain, granite tors, blanket bogs, and rich archaeological history, including Neolithic tombs and Bronze Age stone circles

These pictures are from my training for the south west Ten Tors youth challenge, where I took part with both the 35 and 45 mile teams
